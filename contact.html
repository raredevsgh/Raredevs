Let's Start Building

Here are some quick answers to the most common things clients ask before we begin building together.

How do I start a project with Rare Devs?

Simply reach out through our contact form or email. We’ll schedule a quick discovery call to learn about your vision, goals, and timeline — then propose the best way to make it happen.

What industries do you work with?

We collaborate with startups, brands, and organizations across different sectors — from eCommerce and logistics to healthcare, education, agencies, and more. If there’s a problem worth solving, we’re in.

What services does Rare Devs offer?

We design and develop intuitive digital products including websites, mobile apps, software systems, and custom digital solutions built around your business goals.

How long does a project typically take?

Timelines vary depending on scope and complexity — small projects may take a few weeks, while larger platforms can span a few months. We’ll provide a clear roadmap before development begins.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely. We offer maintenance, feature improvements, and long-term support to keep your product secure, scalable, and evolving with your users.
